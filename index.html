Geometry & Arithmetic of Surfaces Workshop

We will hold a workshop at the University of Wisconsin - Madison, February 9-10, 2019, providing a diverse group of early career researchers the opportunity to learn about specific topics in the arithmetic and algebraic geometry of surfaces from a diverse set of prominent active researchers.

This workshop is organized in Cooperation with AWM by Juliette Bruce, Wanlin Li, and Daniel Erman, and is only possible thanks to the generous support of Melanie Matchett Wood, the National Science Foundation RTG program (DMS-1502553), and the UW Math Department.
